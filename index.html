    What do you need to create a website?

    #1

    Define Your Purpose

    ✍ Determine the main goal or purpose of your website. Do you want to create a personal blog, an online store, a portfolio, or a business website? What is the main message or action you want your visitors to take? Knowing your purpose will guide your decisions throughout the process.

    ✍ Describe your target audience or user demographic. Who are your ideal website visitors, and what are their needs and preferences?

    #2

    Design and Branding

    ✍ Share your branding guidelines, such as colors, fonts, and logos. Discuss your preferred design style and any specific design inspirations you have in mind.

    ✍ If applicable, provide examples of competitor websites that you like or dislike and explain why.

    #3

    Content and Features

    ✍ Outline the content you want on your website, such as text, images, videos, and any specific features.

    ✍ Mention if you need a blog, e-commerce functionality, contact forms, user registration, or any other special features.

    #4

    Other

    ✍ Mention if you already have a domain name and hosting, or if you need assistance with these.

    ✍ Detail any specific functionality you require

    ✍ Discuss any SEO (Search Engine Optimization) requirements, such as keyword targeting, meta tags, and content optimization.

    Remember that open and clear communication is key to a successful web development project. Be prepared to collaborate with the web developer to refine your vision and create a website that meets your goals and expectations.
